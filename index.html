Scan & Sort
About the Project

Scan & Sort is an AI-powered application designed to classify recyclable items using a modified ResNet-50 model. It works offline and supports 15 different item classes. The model was trained using images over the internet and self-acquired images totaling to 15,000 images. 15 classes include: glasses, including aseptic cartons, batteries, broken glass, cardboard, flexible plastic packaging, glass bottles, metal cans, mugs, paper waste, plastic bags, plastic bottles, plastic jugs, plastic utensils, stained cardboard, and styrofoam, with each category containing 1,000 images. The application was targeted to spread awareness on what items can be recycled and promote recycling.

Deepfake Audio Detector for Filipino Language
About the Project

The study aimed to jump-start research on deepfake audio detection in the Filipino language and as a result, led to the development of this prototype. By experimenting and assessing different approaches (OpenSMILE feature extraction, Spectrogram Images, and Transformer models).
We have found great success in the Spectrogram approach with Deep learning and Transfomer models (100% accuracy on Validation data). However, further assessment is required to learn the applicability of each model in the real world.
To make this prototype, Flask was used in the backend to run models and a simple CSS, HTML and JS were used in the front end. Kindly Contact me if you'd like to use the dataset, models and or prototype.
